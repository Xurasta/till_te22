Krabby Patty Secret Formula!

Recipe

You Will Need:

1 Crab Meat

1 Pickle

1 Burger Bun

1 Slice Of Cheese

If you take your 1 Crab Meat and make a patty out of it, put it on the grill for how long you want.

Put it in between your Burger Buns with a slice of Cheese and 1 whole pickle.

Now you're done. Enjoy your own homemade Krabby Patty.
